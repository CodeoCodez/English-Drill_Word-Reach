# English-Drill_Word-Reach
Word Reach is a fast-paced word retrieval game designed to train recall rather than vocabulary knowledge.
The user will choose a starting letter and a category, then race against the clock to generate as many valid words as possible. Each entry is checked against a dictionary to verify that it exists and matches the selected category.
Unlike traditional word games, Word Reach focuses on how quickly you can access words you already know. The game tracks your responses over time, highlights first-time discoveries, and encourages expanding your ability to retrieve words from memory under pressure.

Features
- Choose any letter from A–Z or get a random challenge.
- Practice with categories such as nouns, verbs, adjectives, adverbs, animals, and food & drink.
- Real-time dictionary validation.
- Personal word history stored locally in your browser.
- Timed rounds (30, 60, or 90 seconds).
- Tracks valid words, repeats, and new additions to your personal lexicon.
